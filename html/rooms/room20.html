The Revelation

As you step into the final chamber of the Orokin Derelict, the air hums with a palpable energy. Before you lies the source of all the mysteries and challenges you've faced - a relic of immense power, glowing with an inner light that seems to pulse in time with your own heart.

This is no ordinary artifact; it's a piece of lost Orokin technology, its purpose as enigmatic as the civilization that created it. You realize that this relic holds the key to untold power, capable of shifting the balance in the system. Its discovery could ignite a war among the factions, or it could bring a new era of understanding.

As a Tenno, the weight of this decision rests on your shoulders. Do you take the relic and use its power to shape the future? Do you destroy it, fearing its potential for destruction? Or do you leave it hidden, guarding its secret, as you continue your eternal vigil?

The chamber around you seems to hold its breath, waiting for your decision. In this moment, you're not just a warrior of the Void; you're a guardian of fate, a decider of destinies. Your choice will echo through the corridors of time, shaping the future of the Warframe universe.

  • [Take the Relic]: Harness the relic's power for the greater good, knowing the path ahead will be fraught with new challenges and moral quandaries.
  • [Destroy the Relic]: Eliminate the potential threat it poses, ensuring it never falls into the wrong hands, but at the cost of losing its knowledge forever.
  • [Guard the Secret]: Leave the relic hidden, maintaining the balance as it is, as you silently watch over the ever-shifting tides of power in the system.

Whatever your choice, your journey through the Echoes of the Void has forever changed you. You emerge from the Orokin Derelict, not just as a survivor, but as a legend, a Tenno who faced the unknown and made a choice that would define an era.
